We are Oil Field Supplies & Equipment

Transforming
With Innovations

Oil Field Supplies & Equipment Pte Ltd (OSE) is a Singapore-based company which has, since its inception in 2008, established a clear reputation as a most reliable and trusted supplier of high-quality fishing, milling, drilling as well as specialty tools for the global oil and gas industry.

Our strategy is investing in automation and technology for its tool manufacturing facilities and processes, train and develop a highly technical workforce with capabilities to design and build a wide range of standard and customised fishing tools for our global customers.

OSE’s ability to produce high-quality fishing tools and deliver exceptional service has gained the trust and confidence of the world’s leading fishing services providers. Satisfied customers often cite top quality products, prompt delivery, excellent product knowledge, and capacity to design and custom-build tools as reasons for their continued partnerships with OSE. 

OSE’s vision is be the best-in-class service provider for the global oil and gas industry by providing high-quality downhole equipment through innovative solutions, teamwork, and dependable, uncompromising customer service.

Why Work With Us

We are internationally accredited

OSE's manufacturing facility is API and ISO 9001 2008 accredited with the full range of API gauges. All products are therefore manufactured according to API standards and specifications.

We are a globally competitive company

OSE is on par with suppliers from all over the world. Our appointment as the approved supplier/vendor to the world's top oilfield service providers attests to our our reputation for quality products and exceptional service. We have also received positive feedback and commendations from our global customers.

We have a highly skilled technical workforce

More than 25% of our total workforce have highly specialized skills in design engineering (CAD/CAM), research and development, and software design and programming. OSE is committed to and invests in training its current and new employees in new technologies.

We provide high value, complex manufacturing capabilities

OSE operates a technically advanced manufacturing facility that uses automation and adopts cutting-edge technology to develop a superior range of fishing, drilling and specialty tools for the oil and gas inductry. Our plant engineers work closely in tandem with the Quality Control team to ensure all manufacturing processes strictly adhere to international best practices. Our focus on standards ensures that our customers receive quality products every time.

We provide fast solutions to complex problems

Our engineering team has over 30 years of combined experience in designing and configuring the right tools that provide fast and cost-effective solutions for companies that have broken or lost equipment. Our technically-trained sales consultants are also available to customers to tap their wide experience and deep expertise.

We offer attractive pricing to our global customers

Our operating costs are lower than our larger competitors, so we can offer a wider selection of fishing, milling, P&A, and drilling tools at more competitive prices.

We have a proven track record

We have a long list of customer referrals and repeat orders that attest to our success at supplying quality downhole tools of varying complexity.

Recognition and accolades from satisfied global clients

Fast turnaround, consistently high quality products, responsiveness to customer queries, prompt delivery, and in-depth product knowledge, are some of the reasons why we continue to be the preferred vendor for major global oil services companies.
